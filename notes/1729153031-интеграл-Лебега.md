# Интеграл Лебега
## Definition
$$
\int_X{f(x) \d \mu(x)} \ldefeq \sum_{i=1}^{n}{a_i \mu(E_i)} \in [0, +\infty]
$$
$E \in \mcM$
 $$
\int_E{f(x) \d \mu(x)} \ldefeq \sum_{i=1}^{n}{a_i \mu(E_i \cap E)} =
\int_X{(\chi_E f)(x)) \d \mu(x)}
\in [0, +\infty]
$$

### Корректность

## Definition 2

$f \in L_0(X, \mu)$
$f \ge 0$
