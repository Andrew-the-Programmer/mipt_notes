$b$ - ширина щели.
$N$ - кол-во щелей.

$$
J = E_0^2 \frac{\sin^2{y}}{y^2} \frac{\sin^2\group{\frac{kd \sin\theta}{2} N}}{\sin^2\group{\frac{kd \sin\theta}{2}}}
$$

$$
y = \frac{k b \sin\theta}{2}
$$

$$
d \sin\theta = m \lambda
$$
