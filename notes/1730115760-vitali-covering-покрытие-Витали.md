# Vitali covering | покрытие Витали
Let:
1. $E \subseteq \RR[n]$

Then:
$\mcV(E)$ - ***Vitali covering*** of E, if
$$
\forall x \in E, \delta > 0 \hthen \exists B(x, r) \in \mcV(E): r < \delta
$$
