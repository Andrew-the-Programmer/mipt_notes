# Vitali covering theorem
Let:
1. $E \subseteq \RR[n]$
2. $\abs E < +\infty$
3. $F$ - [[1730115760-vitali-covering-покрытие-Витали|Vitali covering]] of E

Then:
$\exists G \subseteq F: \abs G \le \abs \NN$ and:
$$
\Gather{
\LM[n] \left(E \setminus \bigsqcup_{B \in G}{B}\right) = 0
}
$$
