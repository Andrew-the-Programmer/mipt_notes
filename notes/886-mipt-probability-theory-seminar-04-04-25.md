# MIPT probability theory seminar 04-04-25

# Факториальный момент
$$
E x^{[n]} \defeq E \group{\prod_{i=0}^{n-1}{x - i}}
$$

# Geom
$$
\bbP\set{X = k} = q^k p, \quad p+q=1
$$
$$
\Gather{
\bbE X = \frac{q}{p}\\
D X = \frac{q}{p^2}
}
$$
