### 1)

我每天一起床就去刷牙。

每天 - měitiān - every day
起床 - qǐchuáng - get up
刷牙 - shuāyá - brush teeth
wǒ měitiān yī qǐchuáng jiù qù shuāyá。
I brush my teeth as soon as I get up every day.
