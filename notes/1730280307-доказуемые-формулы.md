# доказуемые формулы
$$
\Gather{
A, \lnot A \vdash B \\
A \vdash B \to A \\
\vdash A \to A \\
A \vdash \lnot \lnot A \\
A, \lnot B \vdash \lnot (A \to B) \\
\lnot B \to \lnot A \vdash A \to B \\
}
$$
