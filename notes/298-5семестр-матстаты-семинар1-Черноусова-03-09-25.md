$$
\groupt{\Omega, \mcF, \bbP} \xra{\xi} \groupt{\RR, \mcB, \bbP_\xi}
$$

$$
\hat{F}_n(x) = \hat{F}(x, \vec{X}) \approx F(x)
$$

$$
