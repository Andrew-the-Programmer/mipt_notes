`Assime:`
1. $A \subseteq \RR[n]$
2. $x \in \RR[n]$
3. $B_\varepsilon(x)$ - [[1730025394-шар|ball]]

# Approximate density
$$
d_\varepsilon(x) = \frac{\mu(A \cap B_\varepsilon(x))}{\mu(B_\varepsilon(x))}{}
$$
# Density
$$
d(x) = \lim_{\varepsilon \to 0}{d_\varepsilon(x)}
$$
