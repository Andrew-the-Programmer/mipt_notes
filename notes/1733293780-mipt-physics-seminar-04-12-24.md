## 12.48
![[1733295340.png]]
$$
\alignleft{
Q = 2\pi \frac{W}{\Delta W} \\
\Delta W = N T \\
w = \frac{E^2}{8\pi} \\
E = E_0 \sin{k_y y} \sin{k_z z} e^{-i \omega t} \\
k_y = \frac{\pi}{a}, \quad k_z = \frac{\pi}{b} \\
W = \int_0^a\int_0^b\int_0^l{w\ \d x \d y \d z} = 
\frac{E_0^2}{8\pi} \frac{a b l}{4} \\
\lambda = \frac{c}{\nu} \\
E_0^2 = \frac{16 Q N \lambda}{V c} \\
}
$$
