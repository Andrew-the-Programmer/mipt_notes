# MIPT differential equations lecture 19-02-25

# Lemma
$$
\AlignLeft{
\varphi, f \in C([a,b]) \\
\forall h \in C^1([a,b]): h(a) = h(b) = 0 \hthen
\int_a^b{(h \varphi + {h}^{'})} = 0 \\
\implies \\
1)\ f \in C^1([a,b]) \\
2)\ \varphi = \dv{x}{f}
}
$$
