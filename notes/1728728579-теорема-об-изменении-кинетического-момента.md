# Теорема об изменении кинетического момента
$$
\dv{t}{\vec{{K}_{A}}}= \vec{{{M}_{A}}^{ex}} + m \vec{{v}_{c}} \times \vec{{v}_{A}}
$$
$$
\begin{cases}
\vec{{v}_{A}} = 0 \\
A \equiv C
\end{cases}
\implies \dv{t}{\vec{{K}_{A}}} = \vec{{{M}_{A}}^{ex}}
$$
$$
\begin{gather}
\dv{t}{\vec{{K}_{A}}}= \Dv{t}{ \sum{\vec{{r}_{Ai}} \times m_i \vec{{v}_{i}} }} \\
\end{gather}
$$
