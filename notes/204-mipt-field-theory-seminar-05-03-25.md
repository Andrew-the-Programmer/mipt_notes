# MIPT field theory seminar 05-03-25
4-скорость
$$
\AlignLeft{
S \defeq c \D \tau \\
\d S = c \d \tau = c \d t \sqrt{1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}} \\
\frac{\d S}{c \d t} = \frac{1}{\gamma(t)} \\
x^\mu(t) \to x^\mu(S) \\
u^\mu \defeq \dv{S}{x^\mu} - \textit{4-вектор} \\
({{u}^{'}})^{\mu} = \Lambda_\nu^\mu u^\nu
}
$$
