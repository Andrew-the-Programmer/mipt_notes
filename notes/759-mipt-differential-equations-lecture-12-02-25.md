# Непрерывность
$J \in C(f_0)$
 $$
\forall \varepsilon > 0 \exists \delta > 0 \forall f \in M \hthen
\abs{J(f) - J(f_0)} < \varepsilon
$$
$$
\norm{J(f) - J(f_0)}_{C} \defeq \sup_{x \in X}{\abs{f - f_0}(x)}
$$
$$
\norm{J(f) - J(f_0)}_{{C}^{'}} = \sup_{x \in X}{\abs{f - f_0}(x)} + \sup_{x \in X}{\abs{{f}^{'} - f_0^{'}}(x)}
$$
