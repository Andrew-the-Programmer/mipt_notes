# Параметрические поля
![[1730537177.svg]]
$$
\abs{\Delta L} \ge \frac{1}{2} RT
$$
$$
\alignleft{
\Phi = I L = const \\
W = \frac{\Phi}{2 L} \\
\Delta W = - \frac{\Phi^2}{2 L^2} \Delta L = - \frac{1}{2} I^2 \Delta L \\
\Delta W \ge \frac{1}{2} R I^2 \frac{T}{2}
}
$$
