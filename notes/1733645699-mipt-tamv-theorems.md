# MIPT-tamv-theorems
# Thm 10.9
`Let:`
$L_1 \le_m L_2$
`Then:`
1. $L_2 \in \mcR \implies L_1 \in \mcR$
2. $L_2 \in \mcR\mcE \implies L_1 \in \mcR\mcE$
3. $L_2 \in \co\mcR\mcE \implies L_1 \in  \co\mcR\mcE$
#
$$
\mcR = \mcR\mcE \cap \co\mcR\mcE
$$
